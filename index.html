Why change
what you use every day?

The concept of household toxic-burden reduction holds that small changes in the domestic environment have a significant cumulative impact on long-term health.



Key fact
Humans ingest approximately
5 grams
of plastic per week — the equivalent
of a credit card.

University of Newcastle, 2019 — commissioned by WWF

Teflon pans

A scratched non-stick pan can release up to 2.3 million plastic microparticles into food. — Environmental Science & Technology, 2023

Antimicrobial copper

The EPA certifies copper as the only solid metal with registered antimicrobial properties, able to eliminate over 99.9% of bacteria in under two hours.

Microplastics in arteries

Researchers in the New England Journal of Medicine (2024) found microplastics in arterial plaque, linking them to higher risk of heart attack and stroke.
